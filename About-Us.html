Mission Statement

Hostage Aid Worldwide’s (HAW) mission is to provide humanitarian aid, and support as well as empower current and former hostages and their families in their personal, emotional, physical, and psychological rehabilitation, reentrance to society and building better lives, and efforts to obtain legal services and assistance to seek reparations from governmental authorities in recompense for their adverse and unlawful experiences. HAW aims to increase the general public’s awareness as well as that of private persons/institutes to the plight of victims of hostage violence and unlawful detainment and the moral imperative to assist impacted such individualspersons; HAW will organize, promote, support and engage in activities to foster and encourage mutual awareness, understanding, and appreciation among people of different countries to stimulate dialogue, disseminate information, and facilitate cultural exchange, with a particular focus on social justice and how unlawful detainment, hostage violence, and torture impact human welfare and international relations as well as violate basic human rights; HAW will advocate for the release of hostages through advocacy efforts in collaboration with (or on behalf of) the hostages’ families. In this, HAW intends to compliment the work carried out by other organizations and groupsNGOs that help and advocate for hostages. However, HAW’s approach is uniquely focused ions different in that it will working closely with technology experts to empirically address the hostage taking “business model” and identifying ways to target components of this model with the aim to disrupt its operation. To this end, HAW is pioneering a state-of-the-art data-driven approach to hostage taking as a tool for the non-governmental and intergovernmental organizations including the United Nations, governments, as well as families of hostages in an effort to put an end to hostage taking. Governments have been struggling to deal with the global hostage crisis and HAW’s hostage tracker can be utilized as a technological step to achieve tangible outcomes.
